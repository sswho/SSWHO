Acquisition Criteria

We target established B2B businesses with strong fundamentals and clear growth potential

📍

Location & Industry

  • Orlando area, remote-friendly, or relocatable operations
  • Product-based or B2B service businesses
  • Established operations with 3+ years of proven performance
  • Strong market position with defensible competitive advantages
💰

Financial Profile

  • Target SDE of approximately $1M annually
  • EBITDA margins of 20% or higher
  • Recurring revenue component of 75% or greater
  • Customer concentration below 20% for reduced risk
📈

Growth Potential

  • Clear operational efficiencies ready to be captured
  • Automation and digital transformation opportunities
  • Underutilized sales channels and market expansion potential
  • Scalable business model with room for strategic improvements
